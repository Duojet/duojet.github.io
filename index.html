Greetings!

Welcome to my website. I am currently a student at The Tech Academy in Portland, OR studying front-end and back-end software development. While I am somewhat new to coding, my entire life has been steeped in technology. I have vast experience using computers since my father brought home one of the first Macintosh computers in the 80’s. Being a musician, I remember using rudimentary desktop publishing software to create flyers for the various bands I was in during middle school and high school. Playing music led to recording music, and upon relocating from the midwest to Nashville, I was among the first wave of recording engineers to exclusively use computers for recording music. While I still play music and own a recording studio here in Portland, I’ve decided to shift my focus and learn software development. I look forward to completing the program at the Tech Academy and putting my skills to use in this exciting and growing field. Thanks for reading!
